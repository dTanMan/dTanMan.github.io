So I made spaceship video game music in a weekend.

my friends had a game idea. the player can control two ships at a time in an infinitely scrolling shoot-em-up, and there are four ships to choose from, each with its own kind of bullets.
tying in to the theme of "joined together", the player can also combine the two ships to make even more powerful bullets.

i had a musical idea. what if the music changes based on the selected ship?
